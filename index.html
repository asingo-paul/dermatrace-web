How It Works

Simple to start.
Powerful over time.

DermaTrace is built around one idea: seeing your skin history side by side tells you more than any single check-up ever could.

  • Add a condition — name it, describe it, pick a body location. Done in seconds.
  • Log a photo — snap a photo and it is timestamped automatically into your timeline.
  • Rate your symptoms — a quick severity score gives you a trackable data point each day.
  • Review the history — scroll back through weeks or months of photos and notes at once.
  • Share with your doctor — export a clean report to bring to your next dermatology visit.
